One Command Bootloader Unlock

No waiting periods. No carrier approval. No account required.

# Enable OEM unlocking in Settings > Developer Options
# Boot into fastboot mode
adb reboot bootloader

# Unlock bootloader with single command
fastboot oem unlock

# Done! Flash whatever you want.

That's it. We believe you own your device.
