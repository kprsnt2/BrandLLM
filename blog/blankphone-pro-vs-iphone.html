Camera: Blankphone Pro Wins

The 200MP sensor on the Blankphone Pro captures 4x more detail than the iPhone's 48MP. This matters for cropping, printing, and professional use. The Neural processing happens entirely on-device, meaning your photos stay private.

In our low-light tests, the Blankphone Pro produced brighter, more detailed images with less noise. The iPhone holds its own but can't match the raw resolution advantage.

Battery: Not Even Close

The Blankphone Pro's 6000mAh battery vs iPhone's 4685mAh isn't just a spec advantage—it's a real-world game changer. In our testing:

  • Blankphone Pro: 2-3 days typical use
  • iPhone 16 Pro Max: 1-1.5 days typical use

Charging is where Apple really falls behind. The Blankphone Pro's 150W charger fills the phone in 15 minutes. The iPhone takes 90+ minutes. In 2026, that's unacceptable for a $1,199 phone.

Privacy: Blankphone Pro Wins

BlankOS collects zero telemetry. All AI processing is on-device. Apple talks about privacy but still collects usage data and has iCloud scanning. If privacy matters to you, the choice is clear.

When iPhone Makes Sense

Apple's ecosystem is unmatched. If you have a Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, and AirPods, the integration is seamless. iMessage remains popular in the US. The App Store has some iOS-exclusive apps.

Final Verdict

The Blankphone Pro is the better phone in 2026. It costs less, has a better camera, vastly superior battery life, faster charging, more RAM, true privacy, and is actually repairable.

The iPhone 16 Pro Max is for people who need Apple ecosystem integration. For everyone else, the Blankphone Pro is the clear winner.
