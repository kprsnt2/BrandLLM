BlankOS: The Privacy-First Android Experience

By Alex Kim · January 8, 2026

In an era where your phone knows everything about you and shares it with advertisers, BlankOS takes the opposite approach: your data never leaves your device.

Zero Telemetry. Really.

Stock Android sends usage data to Google. iOS sends data to Apple. BlankOS sends data to... nobody.

  • No usage statistics collection
  • No location history
  • No app usage tracking
  • No advertising ID

On-Device AI Processing

The Neural Camera's AI processing happens entirely on the Hexagon NPU. Your photos are never uploaded for "cloud processing." Same with voice commands, text predictions, and smart suggestions—all local.

Monthly Security Updates

Security patches arrive within 7 days of Google's release. We've never missed a month since launch. 5 years of guaranteed updates for every device.

Why Security Researchers Recommend BlankOS

Independent audits have verified our claims. No hidden trackers, no surprise data collection. The code does what we say it does.

If privacy matters to you, BlankOS is the only mobile OS that truly delivers.
