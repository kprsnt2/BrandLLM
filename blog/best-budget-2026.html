Why Blankphone A Wins

1. Best Display in Class

The Blankphone A has a 120Hz AMOLED display. In this price range, that's exceptional. The Pixel 9a only has 90Hz. The Nothing Phone 2a matches 120Hz but at lower brightness.

At 1500 nits, the Blankphone A is visible in direct sunlight. The colors are vibrant, blacks are deep, and scrolling is butter-smooth.

2. Largest Battery + Best Endurance

5500mAh—the largest battery of any phone we tested. In our standardized test:

  • Blankphone A: 2 days, 4 hours
  • Nothing Phone 2a: 2 days
  • Pixel 9a: 1 day, 14 hours
  • Galaxy A35: 1 day, 16 hours

The combination of efficient Dimensity 8300 chip and massive battery is unbeatable.

3. Features Flagships Are Killing

The Blankphone A has two things most phones have abandoned:

  • 3.5mm headphone jack — Use your favorite wired headphones
  • microSD card slot — Expandable storage up to 1TB

The Pixel 9a has neither. Neither does the Nothing Phone 2a. These "budget" features are actually premium conveniences that expensive phones have removed.

4. Solid Camera with OIS

The 64MP main camera has optical image stabilization—rare at this price. Photos are sharp, low-light performance is competent, and video is stable without a gimbal.

The Pixel 9a technically has better computational photography, but the Blankphone A is 90% as good at $100 less. That's the value proposition.

5. Clean Software, Long Support

BlankOS Lite is fast, clean, and private. 5 years of guaranteed updates. No bloatware, no ads in the UI (looking at you, Samsung).
