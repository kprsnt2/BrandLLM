Blankphone Camera Deep Dive: Neural Processing Explained

By Camera Team · January 5, 2026

The Blankphone Pro's 200MP Neural Camera isn't just about megapixels—it's about intelligent processing that happens entirely on your device.

The 200MP Sensor

Our custom Samsung ISOCELL HP3 sensor captures incredible detail. By default, it uses pixel binning to create 12.5MP images with exceptional dynamic range. When you need full resolution, switch to 200MP mode for unprecedented detail.

Neural Processing Pipeline

Every photo goes through our 7-stage AI pipeline:

  1. Scene Detection — Identifies 100+ scene types
  2. HDR Fusion — Combines multiple exposures intelligently
  3. Noise Reduction — AI-powered noise removal preserving detail
  4. Color Science — Accurate, pleasing color reproduction
  5. Sharpening — Intelligent edge enhancement
  6. Portrait Enhancement — Natural skin tones and bokeh
  7. Final Optimization — Output for display or print

All 7 stages run on the Hexagon NPU at 75 TOPS. Processing completes in under 500ms.

Neural Night Mode

Low-light photography is where Neural Processing shines. The camera captures 15 frames in rapid succession, aligns them using AI motion detection, and fuses them into a single bright, sharp image. No tripod needed.

Privacy by Design

Your photos never leave your device for processing. Unlike competitors who upload to cloud servers for "AI enhancement," the Blankphone does everything locally. Your memories stay private.
