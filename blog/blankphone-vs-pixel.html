Camera: Blankphone Wins on Hardware

The Blankphone has a 108MP sensor vs Pixel's 50MP, plus a 32MP telephoto the Pixel lacks entirely. Raw photo quality favors Blankphone.

Google's computational photography is excellent, but the Blankphone's Neural processing is equally capable. The hardware advantage tips this to Blankphone.

Performance: Blankphone Wins

Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 vs Tensor G4 isn't close. The Snapdragon is faster, more efficient, and runs cooler. Google's Tensor chips are good for AI tasks but lag in traditional performance benchmarks.

Charging: Blankphone Destroys Pixel

100W vs 27W. The Blankphone charges nearly 4x faster. Same 30-minute charge time gets you:

  • Blankphone: 100% (actually full)
  • Pixel 9: ~50%

Privacy: Blankphone Wins

Google's entire business model is data collection. BlankOS collects nothing. If you care about privacy, this alone makes Blankphone the choice.

When Pixel Makes Sense

If you want 7 years of updates, prefer Google's AI features (Gemini integration, call screening), or want the pure Google experience.

Verdict: Blankphone Wins

Same price, but the Blankphone has: better camera hardware, faster processor, way faster charging, bigger battery, and actual privacy. The Pixel 9 is a good phone, but the Blankphone is better in almost every way that matters.
