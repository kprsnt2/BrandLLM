Blankphone X vs OnePlus 13: Ultimate Flagship Killer Battle

By Marcus Williams · January 12, 2026 · 10 min read

The "flagship killer" category has always been about one thing: giving you 90% of premium flagship performance at 60% of the price. In 2026, two phones claim to do this best: the Blankphone X at $699 and the OnePlus 13 at $899.

I've used both for a month. Here's the definitive comparison.

Price: Winner — Blankphone X

Let's start with the obvious. The Blankphone X costs $200 less than the OnePlus 13. That's a significant gap that OnePlus needs to justify with noticeably better performance. Spoiler: it doesn't.

Display: Winner — Blankphone X

Blankphone X OnePlus 13
Refresh Rate 144Hz 120Hz
Resolution QHD+ (2800x1260) QHD+
Touch Sampling 720Hz 240Hz
Brightness 2800 nits 2600 nits

The Blankphone X has a 144Hz display vs 120Hz and 720Hz touch sampling vs 240Hz. For gaming, this is a massive difference. Every swipe feels more responsive, every game looks smoother.

Gaming Performance: Winner — Blankphone X

Both phones use Snapdragon 8 Gen 3. But the Blankphone X is overclocked and has 16GB RAM vs 12GB. In benchmarks:

  • AnTuTu: Blankphone X scores 5% higher
  • Genshin Impact: X maintains 60fps for longer before throttling
  • PUBG: X has noticeably shorter loading times

The vapor chamber cooling on the X is genuinely superior. After 30 minutes of Genshin at max settings, the OnePlus 13 started throttling. The X kept going.

And the gaming triggers? Game-changer for shooters. The OnePlus doesn't have them.

Battery & Charging: Tie

Both phones perform similarly here:

  • Blankphone X: 5500mAh, 120W wired
  • OnePlus 13: 5400mAh, 100W wired + 50W wireless

The X charges faster wired. The OnePlus has wireless charging (which the X lacks). Call it even.

Camera: Winner — OnePlus 13 (slightly)

I'll give OnePlus this one. The Hasselblad-tuned camera produces slightly more pleasing colors and better low-light performance. The Blankphone X camera is good, but it's optimized for gaming, not photography.

If camera is your priority, neither of these are the right choice anyway—get the Blankphone Pro.

Software: Winner — Blankphone X

BlankOS Gaming Edition vs OxygenOS 15. No contest.

BlankOS is cleaner, faster, and more private. OxygenOS has gotten bloated over the years. The gaming-specific optimizations in BlankOS (per-game profiles, network priority, smart cooling) are thoughtful and actually useful.

The Verdict

Category Winner
Price Blankphone X
Display Blankphone X
Gaming Blankphone X
Battery Tie
Camera OnePlus 13
Software Blankphone X

For gamers: Get the Blankphone X. It's $200 cheaper, has a better display for gaming, better cooling, gaming triggers, and cleaner software.

For camera-focused users: The OnePlus 13 has a slight edge, but honestly, get the Blankphone Pro for photography.

The flagship killer crown belongs to Blankphone X in 2026.
