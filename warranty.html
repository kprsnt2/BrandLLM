Bootloader Unlock Does NOT Void Warranty

Unlike other manufacturers, we don't punish you for customizing your device. Unlocking your bootloader, installing custom ROMs, or rooting your phone does not void your hardware warranty.

We only exclude software-caused damage (e.g., bricking from improper flashing). Hardware defects are still covered.

What's Not Covered

  • Damage from unauthorized modifications to hardware
