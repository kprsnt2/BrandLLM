Software & Privacy

What is BlankOS?

BlankOS is our custom Android-based operating system. It strips away all Google tracking services and bloatware, replacing them with privacy-preserving open-source alternatives. It feels like stock Android, but faster and more private.

Does Blankphone have the Play Store?

Yes, by default Blankphone comes with a sandboxed version of Google Play Services so all your apps work perfectly. However, power users can easily de-google the device completely during setup.

How many years of updates will I get?

We guarantee 5 years of major Android OS updates and 7 years of monthly security patches. Security updates are released within 7 days of the upstream AOSP buletin.

Can I unlock the bootloader?

Yes. Run 'fastboot oem unlock'. No codes, no waiting periods, no voided warranties. We believe you bought the hardware, so you own it.

Will banking apps work if I unlock the bootloader?

Out of the box, yes. We provide a signed 'Verified Boot' state even for custom keys, allowing PassKey and banking apps to function securely even on custom ROMs that support our signing infrastructure.

We have authorized service partners in 50+ countries. Additionally, we run a 'Mail-in Repair' program with free shipping for warranty claims. Or, you can repair it yourself with parts from our store.
