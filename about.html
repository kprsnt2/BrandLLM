About Blankphone

Start Blank. End Brilliant.

Our Mission

We believe your phone should work for you, not against you. In a world of planned obsolescence, bloatware, and data harvesting, Blankphone offers something different: Smartphones that respect your privacy, last for years, and can be repaired.

Founded in 2024

Blankphone was founded by a team of engineers frustrated with the smartphone industry. We saw flagship phones getting more expensive while offering less value. We saw companies removing features (headphone jacks, chargers, repair access) and calling it "innovation." We saw privacy becoming an afterthought.

So we built Blankphone to be the phone we wanted to use ourselves.

Our Values

Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. BlankOS collects zero telemetry. All AI processing happens on-device. We don't sell your information because we don't have it.

Right to Repair

We publish full repair manuals. We sell genuine replacement parts. We design for repairability. You own your device—you should be able to fix it.

Sustainability

Recycled materials. Carbon-neutral manufacturing. Tree planted for every phone sold. 5 years of updates means fewer phones in landfills.

Our Products

We make five phones:

  • Blankphone Pro ($1,099) — Our premium flagship with 200MP camera
  • Blankphone ($799) — The balanced flagship for most people
  • Blankphone X ($699) — Gaming-focused flagship killer
  • Blankphone One ($549) — Premium features at mid-range price
  • Blankphone A ($399) — Budget king with flagship specs

Every phone runs BlankOS, receives 5 years of updates, and is designed for easy repair.
